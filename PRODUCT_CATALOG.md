## The Growth System Framework

Every product BGrowth offers вЂ” regardless of category вЂ” is a **Growth
Systemв„ў**. A Growth System is not a course and not a static download. It
is software a person works *through*, in the same seven stages, every
time:

1. **Discover** вЂ” understand what this Growth System is, who it's for,
   and what changes once it's done. This is the "should I start this"
   stage вЂ” a prospective member evaluates the system before committing.
2. **Learn** вЂ” the concepts, context, and background needed before acting
   вЂ” enough to act with confidence, not a full course to sit through
   first.
3. **Plan** вЂ” turn the goal into a concrete, sequenced plan вЂ” the specific
   decisions, numbers, and choices this person's situation requires.
4. **Take Action** вЂ” do the actual work, step by step, in order вЂ” the
   repeatable workflow at the center of the system.
5. **Track Progress** вЂ” see what's done, what's outstanding, and what's
   next, so the system can be picked up and put down without losing the
   thread.
6. **Grow** вЂ” what comes after this system is finished вЂ” the next system,
   the deeper skill, the adjacent goal it unlocks.
7. **Resources** вЂ” templates, guides, calculators, and reference material
   that support the system without being the system itself.

This seven-stage framework is a **permanent BGrowth standard**. Every
Growth System, in every category вЂ” a business launch, a job search, a
language habit, a budget, a wellness goal, a family event вЂ” is authored
against these same seven stages. A new category never earns a new
framework; it reuses this one.

## Category & Product Naming

- **Growth Systemв„ў** is the universal term for anything built on this
  framework, in any category.
- **Business Systemв„ў** is the established, category-specific name for a
  Growth System inside **Business & Entrepreneurship** вЂ” this name
  predates the broader Growth System concept and keeps its existing brand
  equity; it is not being retired. Other categories don't need their own
  category-specific umbrella term unless one earns the same recognition вЂ”
  by default, a system in any other category is simply referred to as a
  Growth Systemв„ў (e.g. "Resume Builderв„ў, a Growth System in Careers &
  Professions").
- Every individual product keeps its own trademarked name вЂ” *Start Your
  Notary Businessв„ў*, *Resume Builderв„ў*, *Budget Plannerв„ў* вЂ” per CLAUDE.md's
  naming conventions.
- **Collections** are curatorial groupings *within* a category (e.g.
  Business & Entrepreneurship's "Launch Collection"). A single Growth
  System can belong to more than one collection вЂ” collections are tags,
  not exclusive folders.
