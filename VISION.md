# BGrowth Vision

**BGrowth is the Operating System for Personal and Professional Growth.**

This document is not a technical specification. It describes the business
— what BGrowth is for, who it serves, where it's going, and how it makes
money. For the full catalog of what BGrowth offers, see
PRODUCT_CATALOG.md. For how the software is built, see ARCHITECTURE.md and
CLAUDE.md.

---

## Mission

Most people who want to grow — in their business, their career, their
finances, their health, their family life, or a skill like a new language
— aren't short on effort. They're short on structure. There is no
shortage of advice for any of these goals. There is a shortage of
something that turns that advice into action: the actual steps, in the
actual order, ready to work through today.

**BGrowth's mission is to empower people to learn, achieve, and grow
through practical, interactive systems that help them succeed in work,
business, and life.**

Growth is the central concept — not entrepreneurship. Starting a business
is one important way a person grows. So is landing a job, learning a
language, getting out of debt, building a habit, finishing a degree,
getting healthy, or organizing a family's life. BGrowth exists to give
each of those a Growth System™: the same proven, step-by-step structure
that a great mentor, coach, or well-run program would give someone,
without the cost or the years it takes to find one.

## Vision

Think in decades, not years.

**Within the decade:** a Growth System™ exists for every meaningful goal a
person can set — professional or personal. Someone deciding to become a
notary, land their first admin role, learn workplace English, get out of
debt, build a morning routine, finish a degree, lose the weight, or plan a
wedding finds the system already waiting for them inside their Workspace,
a community already discussing it in Club, the people and tools they need
surfaced through Find, and an AI accelerant available the moment they're
stuck. Course platforms teach a topic once. BGrowth *is* the goal — software
people work from every day, not content they consume once and forget.

**Across two decades:** BGrowth is the default place a person's growth
happens, in every category of life, and where they eventually become the
next generation's teacher, partner, mentor, or supplier in the category
they mastered. The member who got out of debt becomes the Personal Finance
instructor; the person who built a cleaning business becomes the Business
& Entrepreneurship mentor; the language learner becomes tomorrow's
conversation partner. BGrowth stops being a company people buy *from* and
becomes an economy people build careers, skills, and lives *inside of* —
a graph of members, creators, professionals, partners, and businesses,
across every category of growth, with BGrowth as the connective operating
layer underneath all of it.

**Beyond that:** the goal is for "growing" and "being inside BGrowth" to
become synonymous in the same way "searching" became synonymous with a
single verb — not because BGrowth owns every path to growth, but because
it is the layer people route through to learn it, plan it, work it,
connect around it, discover what they need for it, sell what they've
mastered in it, and be rewarded for staying in it — whatever "it" is.

Entrepreneurship is one important category inside this much larger growth
ecosystem — not the platform itself. See PRODUCT_CATALOG.md for the full
set of Growth Categories BGrowth serves today and plans to serve next.

## The Growth Categories

BGrowth organizes everything it offers into Growth Categories — the
different areas of life a person can grow in. Each category uses the
exact same Growth System™ framework and the exact same Workspace
experience; only the content differs. See PRODUCT_CATALOG.md for the full
catalog, collections, and flagship products in each:

- 💼 **Business & Entrepreneurship** — start, run, grow, and scale a
  business. *(Live today — this repository's current catalog.)*
- 💼 **Careers & Professions** — qualify for jobs, build a career, grow
  professionally.
- 🌍 **Languages** — communicate confidently, starting with English.
- 💰 **Personal Finance** — organize, protect, and grow personal finances.
- 🎯 **Productivity** — build the habits and systems to get more done.
- 🎓 **Education** — succeed academically, from study habits to exam prep.
- ❤️ **Health & Wellness** — build and sustain healthy habits.
- 🏡 **Family & Lifestyle** — organize life's important moments and
  everyday logistics.

## The BGrowth Ecosystem

Alongside the Growth Categories (*what* a person is growing in), BGrowth is
built as one connected ecosystem of products that each own *how* a person
engages — regardless of category. Each is designed to hand off to the
next; a member should never have to leave the ecosystem, create a second
account, or start over to go from one to the next, or from one category to
another:

- 🎓 **BGrowth Academy™** — *Learn.* Courses, certifications, learning
  paths, and education — for any Growth Category, not only business. Where
  a prospective member learns what a goal actually involves and decides
  whether it's the right path, before committing money, time, or
  reputation to it.

- 💼 **BGrowth Workspace™** — *Work.* The primary experience after login,
  and the successor to everything previously called "BGrowth Library."
  Workspace is where every Growth System™, across every category, actually
  lives and runs — alongside Resources™, Templates™, Guides™,
  Calculators™, and Documents™, plus the member's own Progress, Goals,
  Downloads, and Favorites, a "Continue Building" home base, and AI
  Assistance woven directly into the work. Anything a member is *actively
  working on*, in any category, belongs in Workspace. `app.bgrowth` (this
  repository) is the current, foundational implementation of Workspace.

- 👥 **BGrowth Club™** — *Connect.* Community, networking, groups,
  challenges, mentorship, discussions, and events — organized around every
  Growth Category, so a member finds people working on the same goal, not
  only the same industry.

- 📍 **BGrowth Find™** — *Discover.* Find is the discovery platform of the
  BGrowth ecosystem — deliberately **not** a business directory and
  **not** "Yelp." Its job is to surface everything a person needs to find
  for whatever they're growing: people, professionals, businesses,
  companies, freelancers, coaches, consultants, tutors, software,
  equipment, products, partners, local and online communities, events,
  resources — and, in time, AI-driven recommendations across all of the
  above, across every category.

- 🛒 **BGrowth Marketplace™** — *Create & Sell.* The creator economy layer,
  open to any category. Anyone who has mastered a Growth System — a
  business owner, a career coach, a language tutor, a fitness instructor —
  can create and sell their own Growth Systems, courses, templates,
  documents, AI agents, and automations here.

- 📊 **BGrowth App™** — *Manage.* A full business management platform —
  CRM, finance, projects, scheduling, operations, marketing, inventory,
  HR, reporting, and industry-specific modules. App remains specific to
  the Business & Entrepreneurship category: it's the operational software
  a growing *business* runs on, once its needs outgrow a single Business
  System. It is not a rename of Workspace and not a generic tool for every
  Growth Category.

- 🤖 **BGrowth AI™** — *Accelerate.* Industry and life-area AI assistants —
  a Business Coach, Marketing AI, Sales AI, Financial AI, Customer Support
  AI, Business Planning AI, and equivalents for other categories over time
  — available everywhere a member is working, not siloed in a single chat
  window.

- 🤝 **BGrowth Partners™** — *Partner.* The official partner network —
  companies, brands, and software with affiliate relationships,
  integrations, exclusive offers, educational partnerships, technology
  partnerships, and future strategic partnerships — spanning every Growth
  Category, not only business suppliers.

- 🎁 **BGrowth Rewards™** — *Earn.* Points, achievements, badges, levels,
  and rewards for community participation, referrals, creating, and
  partnering, across every category a member is active in.

- 💎 **BGrowth Benefits™** — *Save.* Exclusive discounts, coupons, partner
  promotions, software discounts, member perks, and savings — for
  business tools as much as for language courses, gym memberships, or
  family expenses.

Together, these form one growth operating system: **learn** in the
Academy, **work** in the Workspace, **connect** in Club, **discover**
through Find, **create and sell** in the Marketplace, **manage** a growing
business in App, **accelerate** every decision with AI, **partner**
through the Partners network, **earn** through Rewards, and **save**
through Benefits — across every Growth Category a member cares about, not
just one.

## Business Philosophy

1. **Growth Systems, not courses.** The Growth System™ — of which the
   Business System™ is the Business & Entrepreneurship category's
   established name — remains BGrowth's core unit of value inside
   Workspace, in every category. A course explains a concept once, on
   video, and leaves the learner to translate it into practice alone. A
   Growth System is software a member works *from* — every time they
   pursue the goal, not once, passively, before forgetting it.
2. **Software you work from, not a document you read.** Every Growth
   System runs live in the browser inside Workspace — a member fills it
   out, prints it, or saves a copy — not a static PDF someone has to
   interpret and adapt themselves.
3. **One system shape, every category.** The same underlying Growth
   System shape — a bundle of Planners™, Workflows™, and Toolkits™,
   authored against the same seven-stage framework (see
   PRODUCT_CATALOG.md) — should serve a notary and a job seeker and a
   language learner and a new parent equally well. Category-specific
   content, not category-specific software.
4. **One member, one identity, every product and category.** A person is
   never a "Workspace user" in one category and a different identity in
   another. The same account, the same progress, and the same reputation
   follow a member across every product and every Growth Category.
5. **Every member is a potential creator, professional, or partner — in
   any category.** BGrowth does not treat "customer" as a permanent, final
   role, and it does not assume that role only exists in business. The
   person who paid off their debt can teach Personal Finance; the person
   who passed their exam can mentor in Education; the person who built a
   cleaning business can mentor in Business & Entrepreneurship.
6. **Discovery and community reinforce commerce; they don't replace it.**
   Find and Club exist to put the right person, professional, or product
   in front of a member at the right moment, in whatever category they're
   growing in — not as separate engagement features, but as the connective
   tissue that makes Marketplace, Academy, and App transactions happen
   naturally instead of through cold outreach.
7. **Trust compounds across the ecosystem and across categories.** A
   member who trusts BGrowth enough to buy one Growth System should find
   every adjacent product — Club community, Find recommendations,
   Marketplace creators, AI guidance, Partners offers, Rewards, and
   Benefits — waiting for them inside the same experience, in any category
   they turn to next.

## Customer Journey

A person's relationship with BGrowth is not a single funnel that ends in
"customer," and it is not specific to any one Growth Category. It is a set
of roles a person can grow into, each unlocking more of the ecosystem,
none of them mutually exclusive, and all available in any category:

1. **Visitor** — discovers BGrowth through Find, an Academy article, a
   specific Growth System, or word of mouth from someone already in Club
   — often searching for their exact goal ("start a notary business,"
   "learn workplace English," "build a budget").
2. **Customer** — buys a single Growth System outright (lifetime access,
   no subscription required) after evaluating it on its product page:
   what's included, what it costs, proof it works.
3. **Member** — joins BGrowth Club for member pricing across every system
   in every category, community access, and ongoing engagement — the
   point where BGrowth stops being a one-time purchase and starts being an
   ongoing relationship, centered on Workspace as their home base.
4. From Member, a person's path can branch into any (or several) of:
   - **Professional** — gets discovered through Find and hired or engaged
     by other members for their expertise, in any category.
   - **Business Owner** — outgrows a single Business System and moves into
     BGrowth App to manage a growing operation — CRM, finance, projects,
     team.
   - **Creator** — builds and lists their own Growth Systems, templates,
     or automations in Marketplace, in any category.
   - **Instructor** — teaches what they've mastered through Academy
     courses and certifications, in any category.
   - **Marketplace Seller** — sells digital products at any scale, from a
     single template to a full catalog.
   - **Community Leader** — runs groups, challenges, or mentorship inside
     Club, around any goal.
   - **Partner** — becomes part of the official BGrowth Partners network,
     offering products, integrations, or services to members directly.
   - **Ambassador** — refers new members into the ecosystem, earning
     through Rewards for doing so.
5. **Repeat, at every level, across categories** — because BGrowth's roles
   compound rather than replace each other, and because a person's growth
   rarely stays in one category, a person can be a Member in Business, a
   Customer in Personal Finance, a Creator in Languages, and an Ambassador
   throughout — all inside the same account, all reinforcing why they
   stay.

## Revenue Model

- **Subscriptions** — BGrowth Club membership and Workspace/App tiers:
  recurring revenue for member pricing, ongoing access, and deeper
  ecosystem features, across every category.
- **Growth Systems** — individual, one-time Growth System sales (Business
  Systems today; every other category's systems as they launch), lifetime
  access, no subscription required. The lowest-commitment way to buy.
- **Academy revenue** — course and certification sales, standalone or
  bundled with Club membership, across every category.
- **Software subscriptions** — BGrowth App tiers for businesses that need
  ongoing CRM, finance, ops, and reporting tooling beyond a single
  Business System.
- **Marketplace commissions** — a share of every Growth System, course,
  template, document, AI agent, or automation sold by creators through
  Marketplace's creator economy, in any category.
- **Affiliate commissions** — revenue from trusted third-party products
  and services recommended contextually inside Growth Systems, Find
  results, and Benefits offers, across every category.
- **Partner sponsorships** — placement, integration, and visibility fees
  from companies in the BGrowth Partners network, surfaced through Find,
  Marketplace, and Benefits.
- **Advertising** — sponsored placement for relevant products, services,
  and professionals inside Find and Academy, kept clearly distinguishable
  from organic recommendations.
- **Future enterprise products** — team, franchise, and agency tiers of
  BGrowth App for businesses operating at a scale a single-member account
  no longer fits.
- **Free tier as an acquisition engine** — free Growth Systems, free
  Academy content, free Find discovery, and Benefits savings exist to
  build trust and audience ahead of a purchase, not to monetize directly.

## Long-Term Roadmap

**Phase 1 — Prove the core (current phase).** A static, browser-run
Growth System Runtime with a real catalog in one category — Business &
Entrepreneurship — plus the first implementation of the Workspace shell
itself: sign-in-free today, but already shaped as the permanent home for
Continue Building, My Business Systems, recommendations, and a member's
profile. Sold individually and through an early Club offering. This is
where `app.bgrowth` is today.

**Phase 2 — Studio & content scale.** BGrowth Studio becomes the internal
tool that lets BGrowth (and eventually partners and creators) author new
Growth Systems — Planners, Workflows, Toolkits, Templates — without
hand-writing data files, and export them into Workspace. Catalog breadth
expands within Business & Entrepreneurship first, proving the model before
expanding categories.

**Phase 3 — Accounts, persistence, and real commerce.** Real
authentication, saved member progress, real checkout and membership
billing, and a genuine Workspace replace today's static placeholders —
without changing what a Growth System *is* to the member.

**Phase 4 — Category expansion.** The remaining seven Growth Categories
(Careers & Professions, Languages, Personal Finance, Productivity,
Education, Health & Wellness, Family & Lifestyle — see PRODUCT_CATALOG.md)
launch on the same Runtime and Workspace proven in Phase 1–3, alongside
Academy, Club, Find, Marketplace, AI, Partners, Rewards, and Benefits
coming online as connected products around the same account — each
reinforcing the others rather than operating as separate silos.

**Phase 5 — Platform & creator economy.** Marketplace opens fully to
independent creators, instructors, and professionals across every
category; Partners scales into a genuine network business; BGrowth App
matures into a real CRM/operations platform for the Business &
Entrepreneurship category; Rewards and Benefits mature into a full loyalty
economy — BGrowth becomes the platform other growth businesses are built
on, not only a product itself.

**Beyond the roadmap** — see Vision above: the decade-and-beyond outcome
this roadmap is building toward.

## Future Platform Evolution

Every product in the ecosystem is designed to plug into the same member,
the same account, and the same catalog of Growth Systems — not to be
rebuilt as a separate app with its own login and its own data, and not to
be rebuilt per Growth Category either. As BGrowth grows, **Workspace**
remains the place work actually gets done — running Growth Systems in any
category, tracking progress, getting AI assistance in the moment. Academy,
Club, Find, Marketplace, AI, Partners, Rewards, and Benefits all exist to
get the right person, with the right context, into the right Growth
System inside Workspace, and to support them once they're there — whether
that system is a business launch, a job search, or a wedding plan.
**BGrowth App** remains the one product scoped specifically to the
Business & Entrepreneurship category — where a member goes when their
*business* has outgrown what a Business System alone can manage — a
distinct, later step, not a rename of Workspace and not a pattern to repeat
for every other category.
