# BGrowth Vision

**BGrowth is the operating system for business growth.**

This document is not a technical specification. It describes the business
— what BGrowth is for, who it serves, where it's going, and how it makes
money. For how the software is built, see ARCHITECTURE.md and CLAUDE.md.

---

## Mission

Most people who start or grow a business aren't short on effort — they're
short on structure. There is no shortage of advice about business. There is
a shortage of something that turns that advice into action: the actual
steps, in the actual order, ready to work through today, plus the people,
community, and tools around it that make those steps stick.

BGrowth's mission is to give anyone building a business — whatever their
profession, whatever stage they're at, whatever role they eventually grow
into — the complete operating system a franchise would give its owners,
without the franchise fees, the territory restrictions, or the years it
takes to build that structure alone:

- Proven **Business Systems™** to run the business day to day.
- A place to **learn** the profession before committing money or time.
- A **community** of people doing the same work.
- A way to **discover** the people, professionals, and products a business
  actually needs.
- A **marketplace** to create and sell what they've mastered, once they
  have.
- **Software** to manage the business as it scales past what a Business
  System alone can hold.
- **AI** that accelerates every decision along the way.
- A network of **partners**, **rewards**, and **savings** that make
  staying inside the ecosystem worth more than leaving it.

This is no longer a mission about one digital product. It is a mission
about the entire lifecycle of building a business — and every product in
the BGrowth ecosystem exists to serve one part of that lifecycle, connected
to all the others.

## Vision

Think in decades, not years.

**Within the decade:** a Business System™ exists for every profession on
earth — chosen, not built from scratch. Someone deciding to become a
notary, launch a cleaning company, or start bookkeeping for local
businesses finds the system already waiting for them inside their
Workspace, the community already discussing it in Club, the people and
tools they need surfaced through Find, and an AI accelerant available the
moment they're stuck. Course platforms teach a topic once. BGrowth *is* the
business — software people work from every day, not content they consume
once and forget.

**Across two decades:** BGrowth is the default place a business is born,
run, grown, and eventually where its owner becomes the next generation's
teacher, partner, mentor, or supplier. The customer of today becomes the
Academy instructor, Marketplace creator, Find-listed professional, or
Partners-network company of tomorrow — not as an exception, but as the
expected arc of staying in the ecosystem long enough. BGrowth stops being a
company people buy *from* and becomes an economy people build careers and
companies *inside of* — a graph of members, creators, professionals,
partners, and businesses, all transacting, teaching, hiring, and
recommending each other, with BGrowth as the connective operating layer
underneath all of it.

**Beyond that:** the goal is for "growing a business" and "being inside
BGrowth" to become synonymous in the same way "searching" became
synonymous with a single verb — not because BGrowth owns every business,
but because it is the layer every business, professional, and partner
routes through to find each other, learn from each other, sell to each
other, and manage what they've built.

## The BGrowth Ecosystem

BGrowth is a single company built as one connected ecosystem, not ten
separate apps. Each product below owns one verb in a member's life, and
each is designed to hand off to the next — a member should never have to
leave the ecosystem, create a second account, or start over to go from one
to the next.

- 🎓 **BGrowth Academy™** — *Learn.* Courses, certifications, learning
  paths, and business education. Where a prospective business owner or
  professional learns what a profession or skill actually involves, earns
  the credentials that prove it, and decides whether it's the right path
  — before committing money, time, or reputation to it.

- 💼 **BGrowth Workspace™** — *Work.* The primary experience after login,
  and the successor to everything previously called "BGrowth Library."
  Workspace is where interactive Business Systems™ live and run, alongside
  Resources™, Templates™, Guides™, Calculators™, and Documents™ — plus the
  member's own Progress, Goals, Downloads, and Favorites, a "Continue
  Building" home base, and AI Assistance woven directly into the work.
  Anything a member is *actively working on* belongs in Workspace, not
  scattered across other products. `app.bgrowth` (this repository) is the
  current, foundational implementation of Workspace.

- 👥 **BGrowth Club™** — *Connect.* Community, networking, groups,
  challenges, mentorship, discussions, and events. Club is how a member
  stops working alone — finding and being found by other people running
  the same kind of business, at the same stage, with the same questions.

- 📍 **BGrowth Find™** — *Discover.* Find is the discovery platform of the
  BGrowth ecosystem — deliberately **not** a business directory and
  **not** "Yelp." Its job is to surface everything a business needs to
  find, in one place: people, professionals, businesses, companies,
  freelancers, coaches, consultants, software, equipment, products,
  partners, local businesses, online businesses, communities, events,
  resources — and, in time, AI-driven recommendations across all of the
  above. Where Club is about the people you already know, Find is about
  everyone and everything you don't yet.

- 🛒 **BGrowth Marketplace™** — *Create & Sell.* The creator economy layer.
  Anyone who has built expertise inside BGrowth — a member, a professional,
  an instructor — can create and sell Business Systems, courses, templates,
  documents, AI agents, Canva templates, worksheets, and automations here.
  Marketplace is also where BGrowth's own catalog and vetted partner
  products are bought and sold today; over time it opens further to
  independent creators.

- 📊 **BGrowth App™** — *Manage.* A full business management platform —
  CRM, finance, projects, scheduling, operations, marketing, inventory, HR,
  reporting, and industry-specific modules. This is distinct from
  Workspace: Workspace is where a member *works through* a Business
  System; App is the operational software a growing business runs *on*,
  once its needs outgrow a single system. A member graduates from
  Workspace into App as their business scales, without switching
  ecosystems or accounts.

- 🤖 **BGrowth AI™** — *Accelerate.* Industry AI assistants — a Business
  Coach, Marketing AI, Sales AI, Financial AI, Customer Support AI, and
  Business Planning AI — available everywhere a member is working, not
  siloed in a single chat window. AI accelerates decisions inside
  Workspace, App, Find, and Marketplace alike, rather than being its own
  destination.

- 🤝 **BGrowth Partners™** — *Partner.* The official partner network —
  companies, brands, and software with affiliate relationships,
  integrations, exclusive offers, educational partnerships, technology
  partnerships, and future strategic partnerships with BGrowth. Partners
  is the supply side that Find helps members discover, that Marketplace
  helps members transact with, and that Benefits turns into savings.

- 🎁 **BGrowth Rewards™** — *Earn.* Points, achievements, badges, levels,
  and rewards for community participation, referrals, creating, and
  partnering. Rewards makes every other product's engagement — finishing
  a Business System, answering a question in Club, referring a member,
  selling in Marketplace — visibly worth something.

- 💎 **BGrowth Benefits™** — *Save.* Exclusive discounts, coupons, partner
  promotions, software discounts, member perks, and business savings.
  Benefits is where Partners' relationships become tangible savings for
  every member, whether or not they've bought anything else.

Together, these form one growth operating system: **learn** in the
Academy, **work** in the Workspace, **connect** in Club, **discover**
through Find, **create and sell** in the Marketplace, **manage** the
business in App, **accelerate** every decision with AI, **partner**
through the Partners network, **earn** through Rewards, and **save**
through Benefits. No product exists in isolation — each is a doorway into
the same member, the same account, and the same growing business.

## Business Philosophy

1. **Systems, not courses.** The Business System™ remains BGrowth's core
   unit of value inside Workspace. A course explains a concept once, on
   video, and leaves the learner to translate it into practice alone. A
   Business System is software a member works *from* — every time they
   run their business, not once, passively, before forgetting it.
2. **Software you work from, not a document you read.** Every Business
   System runs live in the browser inside Workspace — a member fills it
   out, prints it, or saves a copy — not a static PDF someone has to
   interpret and adapt themselves.
3. **One member, one identity, every product.** A person is never a
   "Workspace user" in one place and a "Marketplace seller" in another
   with a separate login. The same account, the same progress, and the
   same reputation follow a member across Academy, Club, Find, Marketplace,
   App, AI, Partners, Rewards, and Benefits.
4. **Every member is a potential creator, professional, or partner.**
   BGrowth does not treat "customer" as a permanent, final role. The
   ecosystem is deliberately built so a member can become an Academy
   instructor, a Marketplace creator, a Find-listed professional, or a
   Partners-network company without leaving — because the person who most
   deeply understands a Business System is often the person who should be
   teaching or selling the next version of it.
5. **Discovery and community reinforce commerce; they don't replace it.**
   Find and Club exist to put the right person, professional, or product
   in front of a member at the right moment — not as separate engagement
   features, but as the connective tissue that makes Marketplace,
   Academy, and App transactions happen naturally instead of through cold
   outreach.
6. **Trust compounds across the ecosystem.** A member who trusts BGrowth
   enough to buy one Business System should find every adjacent product —
   Club community, Find recommendations, Marketplace creators, AI
   guidance, Partners offers, Rewards, and Benefits — waiting for them
   inside the same experience, not sold to them as a disconnected upsell.

## Customer Journey

A person's relationship with BGrowth is not a single funnel that ends in
"customer." It is a set of roles a person can grow into, each unlocking
more of the ecosystem, and none of them mutually exclusive:

1. **Visitor** — discovers BGrowth through Find, an Academy article, a
   specific Business System, or word of mouth from someone already in
   Club — often searching for their exact profession ("start a notary
   business," "cleaning business launch").
2. **Customer** — buys a single Business System outright (lifetime access,
   no subscription required) after evaluating it on its product page:
   what's included, what it costs, proof it works.
3. **Member** — joins BGrowth Club for member pricing across every system,
   community access, and ongoing engagement — the point where BGrowth
   stops being a one-time purchase and starts being an ongoing
   relationship, centered on Workspace as their home base.
4. From Member, a person's path can branch into any (or several) of:
   - **Professional** — gets discovered through Find and hired or engaged
     by other members for their expertise.
   - **Business Owner** — outgrows a single Business System and moves into
     BGrowth App to manage a growing operation — CRM, finance, projects,
     team.
   - **Creator** — builds and lists their own Business Systems, templates,
     or automations in Marketplace.
   - **Instructor** — teaches what they've mastered through Academy
     courses and certifications.
   - **Marketplace Seller** — sells digital products at any scale, from a
     single template to a full catalog.
   - **Community Leader** — runs groups, challenges, or mentorship inside
     Club.
   - **Partner** — becomes part of the official BGrowth Partners network,
     offering products, integrations, or services to members directly.
   - **Ambassador** — refers new members and businesses into the
     ecosystem, earning through Rewards for doing so.
5. **Repeat, at every level** — because running a business is repetitive
   work, and because BGrowth's roles compound rather than replace each
   other, a person can be a Member, a Creator, a Professional, and an
   Ambassador all at once, all inside the same account, all reinforcing
   why they stay.

## Revenue Model

- **Subscriptions** — BGrowth Club membership and Workspace/App tiers:
  recurring revenue for member pricing, ongoing access, and deeper
  ecosystem features.
- **Business Systems** — individual, one-time Business System sales,
  lifetime access, no subscription required. The lowest-commitment way to
  buy.
- **Academy revenue** — course and certification sales, standalone or
  bundled with Club membership.
- **Software subscriptions** — BGrowth App tiers for businesses that need
  ongoing CRM, finance, ops, and reporting tooling beyond a single
  Business System.
- **Marketplace commissions** — a share of every Business System, course,
  template, document, AI agent, or automation sold by creators through
  Marketplace's creator economy.
- **Affiliate commissions** — revenue from trusted third-party products
  and services (bonding, insurance, equipment, software) recommended
  contextually inside Business Systems, Find results, and Benefits offers.
- **Partner sponsorships** — placement, integration, and visibility fees
  from companies in the BGrowth Partners network, surfaced through Find,
  Marketplace, and Benefits.
- **Advertising** — sponsored placement for relevant products, services,
  and professionals inside Find and Academy, kept clearly distinguishable
  from organic recommendations.
- **Future enterprise products** — team, franchise, and agency tiers of
  BGrowth App for businesses operating at a scale a single-member account
  no longer fits.
- **Free tier as an acquisition engine** — free Business Systems, free
  Academy content, free Find discovery, and Benefits savings exist to
  build trust and audience ahead of a purchase, not to monetize directly.

## Long-Term Roadmap

**Phase 1 — Prove the core (current phase).** A static, browser-run
Business System Runtime with a real catalog of systems across a handful of
professions, plus the first implementation of the Workspace shell itself
— sign-in-free today, but already shaped as the permanent home for
Continue Building, My Business Systems, recommendations, and a member's
profile. Sold individually and through an early Club offering. This is
where `app.bgrowth` is today.

**Phase 2 — Studio & content scale.** BGrowth Studio becomes the internal
tool that lets BGrowth (and eventually partners and creators) author new
Business Systems — Planners, Workflows, Toolkits, Templates — without
hand-writing data files, and export them into Workspace. Catalog breadth
expands across many more professions.

**Phase 3 — Accounts, persistence, and real commerce.** Real
authentication, saved member progress, real checkout and membership
billing, and a genuine Workspace replace today's static placeholders —
without changing what a Business System *is* to the member.

**Phase 4 — Ecosystem activation.** Academy, Club, Find, Marketplace, App,
AI, Partners, Rewards, and Benefits come online as connected products
around the same account and the same Business System data, each
reinforcing the others rather than operating as separate silos.

**Phase 5 — Platform & creator economy.** Marketplace opens fully to
independent creators, instructors, and professionals; Partners scales into
a genuine network business; BGrowth App matures into a real CRM/operations
platform; Rewards and Benefits mature into a full loyalty economy — BGrowth
becomes the platform other growth businesses are built on, not only a
product itself.

**Beyond the roadmap** — see Vision above: the decade-and-beyond outcome
this roadmap is building toward.

## Future Platform Evolution

Every product in the ecosystem is designed to plug into the same member,
the same account, and the same catalog of Business Systems — not to be
rebuilt as a separate app with its own login and its own data. As BGrowth
grows, **Workspace** remains the place work actually gets done — running
Business Systems, tracking progress, getting AI assistance in the moment.
Academy, Club, Find, Marketplace, AI, Partners, Rewards, and Benefits all
exist to get the right person, with the right context, into the right
Business System inside Workspace, and to support them once they're there.
**BGrowth App** is where a member goes when their business has outgrown
what a Business System alone can manage — a distinct, later step, not a
rename of Workspace.
